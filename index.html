What is Bitness?

Bitness is a group exercise installation system targeted towards corporate gymnasiums. The overall experience helps to create a workout environment that is both engaging and welcoming to those who are both new and familar to the gym. It is designed to promote a healthy lifestyle and promote a healthy lifestyle and promote group commaraderie within the workplace. We are building health-conscious communities, one company at a time.

Healthy Lifestyles Meet the Workplace

  • Promote Team Building

    Working out in a group helps you build relationships with the others that surround you. This bring you together as a team.

  • Create a Fitness Culture

    By promoting a healthy and active lifestyle, employees will become part of a fitness culture that will allow them to more effectively relieve stress and work more relaxed.

  • Increase Work Output

    Healthy employees are happy employees. If companies are able to keep their employees happy and stress free, productivity levels increase and this increases the bottom line.
